Data description:

The dataset rb_adultcontemp_duplicate.csv contains all lyrical information for genres R and B and Adult contemporary. This includes duplicates when songs occur over multiple weeks on the Billboard charts. 

The dataset rb_adultcontemp.csv contains only unique row entries from the previous dataset, with a random subset representing 5% of the data removed for final testing purposes.  

The dataset rb_adultcontemp_holdout.csv is the five percent random holdout sample from the above dataset.
